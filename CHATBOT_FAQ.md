## Supported Languages

The app is available in 8 languages to serve diverse communities across the United States:

1. **English** 🇺🇸 - Default language
2. **Spanish (Español)** 🇪🇸 - Second most spoken language in the US
3. **Chinese (中文)** 🇨🇳 - Mandarin Chinese
4. **Tagalog** 🇵🇭 - Widely spoken in Filipino communities
5. **Vietnamese (Tiếng Việt)** 🇻🇳 - Common in Vietnamese-American communities
6. **Arabic (العربية)** 🇸🇦 - For Arabic-speaking communities (with RTL support)
7. **French (Français)** 🇫🇷 - For French-speaking communities
8. **Korean (한국어)** 🇰🇷 - For Korean-American communities

To change your language, click the globe icon (🌐) at the top-right corner of the app and select your preferred language. The interface will update instantly!
